FEATURES

Power & Fuel-Efficiency The Tata Yodha range of Pickups is powered with the ever-reliable Tata Motors 2.2L VARICOR Intercooled Turbocharged BS6 DI engine, that delivers best-in-class power and fuel-efficiency. This heavy-duty engine is designed for a power of 74.8 kW (100 HP) @ 3750 r/min and a high torque of 250 Nm at flat curve wider band from 1000 - 2500 r/min. This enables fewer gear shifts and better pickup in loaded conditions. Additionally, a low NVH (Noise - Vibration - Harshness) promises a smooth and comfortable driving experience.
Performance & Ruggedness Go anywhere with the robust, Tata Yodha Pickup that comes with strong and reliable driveline aggregates: Strong suspension, wide rear axle, disc brakes in front and a 4 mm thick-rolled chassis frame, sturdy enough to withstand all loads on the trickiest terrains. With large, 16-inch tyres and a reliable gear box, it delivers a gradeability of up to 40%. A clutch diameter of 260 mm enables smooth gear-shifting and an enhanced clutch life. Add to that, its optimized Gear ratios and rear differential axle gear ratio ensure a high pulling-power and mileage. Plus, a ground clearance of 210 mm allows you take any load on the toughest road conditions.
High Revenue Maximise your profits with the Tata Yodha Pickup that comes with the biggest cargo deck internal loading area of 47.9 sq.ft., along with payload options of 1200 kg and 1700 kg. Its CED-painted high-strength steel body delivers maximum durability. Plus, with its massive 16-inch tyres, it takes all the load on any terrain, effortlessly. Tata Yodha is built strong to perform on all grades, load and road conditions, so that your earnings are maximum.
High on Safety The Tata Yodha Pickup range is built to deliver superior safety for the occupants and goods it carries. It comes with a large bonnet with a crumple zone and a collapsible steering wheel that ensures maximum safety in case of a frontal collision. Anti-roll bars fitted in the front and a wider rear axle track adds to the stability on road and off-road conditions. And its twin-pot disc brakes in the front ensure better braking with any load, on any road condition.
High on Savings The Tata Yodha Pickup range is designed to have the lowest maintenance cost in the category. With a driveline that needs minimum maintenance, it delivers maximum savings and high uptime. With an Engine oil change interval of 20,000 kms, Gear box & rear Differential oil interval of 80,000 kms, an LFL (Lubricated for life) propeller shaft, and a no-greasing-needed feature for hub and suspension means ZERO maintenance, and massive savings for you. Additionally, our 90-min/120-min Express Service promise, along with an exclusive Tata Yodha priority Service helpline number 1800 209 7979 is designed to help you get back on the road as quickly as possible.
High on Comfort & Convenience The Tata Yodha Pickup range is intuitively designed with your needs in mind. Right from its aerodynamic design, SUV-type tall stance to a muscular cabin design and trendy decals on side doors, it makes its imposing presence felt on the road. Add to that, a bunch of impressive features like a tiltable & collapsible power steering, comfortable bucket seats with headrests, rear sliding windows ensuring cabin ventilation, a stylish dashboard with utility spaces, inbuilt fast mobile charger, bottle holder, news paper pocket, lockable glove box, wide ORVM for enhanced visibility, and a Reverse Parking Assistance System (RSPS) to assist customer while reversing, the Tata Yodha range of Pickups come loaded with features that deliver maximum comforts and convenience.
