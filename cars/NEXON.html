FEATURES





HEAD TURNER DESIGN



New Nexon with its raised hood, sleek lamps and dynamic stance gives it a aggressive SUV style. The signature X factor of Nexon has become futuristic.





SAFETY



New Nexon takes the safety to new level with 6 Airbags, 3 point ELR for all occupants and Electronic stability program as standard.






FUTURISTIC TECHNOLOGY



New Nexon offers latest tech features like first in segment 10.25" Infotainment system by Harman and 10.25" digital Instrument cluster, Connected Vehicle technology with IRA 2.0 and many more.





EXCITING PERFORMANCE



New Nexon outperformance all competition with Turbocharged Petrol and Diesel engine. New Nexon is geared with monostable shifter with paddle shifter in AMT and DCT transmision for refined ergonomics.







PLUSH COMFORT



What was comfortable, became way more comfortable in the New Nexon which comes with leatherette Ventilated Seats, Height Adjustable driver and co-driver seat and many more.
